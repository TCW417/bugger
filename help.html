How To Play

Bugger is a fresh, code based take on an 80's arcade classic. In it, you play as a bug- a broken piece of code- and your goal is to make it to production, and maybe even take a couple of your friends along for the ride. You will be dodging various strings of linter errors to successfully burrow away and wait until launch day.

Use the arrow keys to navigate this treacherous landscape and avoid the linter which will detect your presence! If you bump into a linter error, it will have caught you and the software engineers will remove you from existence. Yikes!

Try to get a high score! Have fun!
